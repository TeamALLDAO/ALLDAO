Explore Daos

1

DSMD

DSMD means Decentralized Social Media DAO

DSMD is a DAO focused on creating a decentralized social media platform that prioritizes user privacy and content ownership. Members can vote on development proposals and features to add to the platform.

2

DI

DI means Decentralized Investment

DI is a DAO focused on making decentralized investments in early-stage blockchain projects. Members can submit investment proposals and vote on which projects to invest in.

3

DGD

DGD means Decentralized Gaming DAO

DGD is a DAO focused on creating decentralized games that prioritize user ownership and participation. Members can vote on which games to develop and which features to add to existing games.

4

DSC

DSC means Decentralized Supply Chain DAO

DSC is a DAO focused on creating a more transparent and decentralized supply chain management system. Members can vote on which supply chain projects to fund and which features to add to the platform.

5

DHC

DHC means Decentralized Healthcare DAO

DHC is a DAO focused on creating a more decentralized and accessible healthcare system. Members can vote on which healthcare projects to fund and which features to add to the platform.

6

DADC

DADC means Digital Art Collectors DAO

DADC is a DAO focused on promoting and collecting digital art. It allows members to vote on which artists to support and which pieces to add to the DAO's collection. DADC members also have access to exclusive NFT drops from the supported artists.
